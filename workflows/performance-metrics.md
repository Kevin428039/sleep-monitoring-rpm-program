# Performance Metrics

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

1. **Sleep Duration Metrics**: 
    - Average sleep duration per patient.
    - Ideal sleep duration compared to actual for improvement tracking.

2. **Sleep Quality Scores**:
    - Analysis of deep vs. light sleep.
    - Regular reporting on sleep disturbances and overall sleep architecture.

3. **Patient Compliance Rates**:
    - Percentage of patients adhering to prescribed sleep programs.
    - Impact of compliance on overall health outcomes.

4. **Dashboard Analytics**:
    - Visual representation of sleep patterns over time.
    - Interactive elements for reported metrics and trend analysis.

5. **Performance Benchmarks**:
    - Establishing standard metrics for comparison against population norms.
    - Quarterly reviews and adjustments based on emerging data and research results.
